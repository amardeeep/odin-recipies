Shrimp Scampi

Description:

This easy shrimp scampi recipe is a simple but elegant shrimp dish. Combine it with garlic bread and a salad for a delicious dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 package angel hair pasta
  • 1 cup dry white whine
  • 1 pound shrimp

Steps:

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook angel hair pasta in the boiling water, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, 4 to 5 minutes. Drain; transfer to a serving bowl and keep warm.
  2. Pour shrimp with sauce over pasta in the serving bowl; toss well. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese and parsley.
